Spring, the time of the year when people wear fancy blouses, and love pink-shaded makeup products that match their outfits. Rose quartz color products especially pop out this spring. It is a must item to create a lovely and fresh vibe.

But no matter how bubbly the color of the product is, one condition needs to be met first to really prove its potential—a neatly ready skin. Without it, the shadowed skin tone presents an overall weary look and the makeup itself would not adhere to the skin, easily coming loose. This is why before putting on make-up, you have to take care of moisture-sebum balance control and dead skin cells. Especially for dry skin types, a more elaborate care is a must, not an option during the spring season. Always remember the spring wind is as scary as winter heaters for dry skins. The following are personal recommendations by a dry skin type editor, who suffers from skin drought every spring.

"
Peel Products for Dead Skin Cells around Your Nose

Sisley Crème Commante Gentle Facial Buffing Cream

A scrub cream that removes dead skin cells and residues with no irritation. Also includes skin tone enhancing effects. Dry and sensitive skins are recommended to use this product once a week, while complex and sensitive skin type are 2-3 times a week.

Primera Facial Mild Peeling

Also mentioned in a Korean cosmetic-related TV program. As the name suggests, the product suits all skin types with mild usage.

"
Moisture-Recharging Items for Dewy and Smooth Skins

Sulwhasoo Overnight Vitalzing Mask Ex

Word of mouth made this oriental medicine sleeping pack famous. Contains walnut extracts with excellent moisturizing effects.

Dr.Jart+ Ceramidin Mask

Dr. Jart’s Cermadin Cream, a product most dry skin types have heard of at least once, is now available as facial masks. Low-irritation nano sheets are a big recommendation for sensitive skins.

Laneige Water Sleeping Pack_ EX

A steady seller among sleeping packs. The light gel type product is better for oily-complex and oily skin types than dry skins.
