This is the era of collaborations, from makeup to fashion brands using cute characters that capture the hearts of fans or creating sophisticated products with famous celebrities.

A sophisticated collaboration by domestic and foreign beauty brands

Beauty brand collaborations are expanding rapidly with the amalgamation of beauty and fashion brands. Here are some products that have benefited from the synergy effect created by collaborations between beauty brands and fashion designers.

Image source : HERA X NICHOLAS KIRKWOOD

Hera x Nicholas Kirkwood

‘Hera’ has solidified its position as a leader in k-beauty and have caught the attention of many for their varied and numerous collaborations. This season, Hera has teamed up with British luxury shoe designer Nicholas Kirkwood to create an intense collection that combines feminine floral patterns and metallic graphic lines. Hera was able to create a synergy effect by combining Nicholas Kirkwood’s signature zigzag lines with feminine, yet bold floral patterns. The ‘Hera UV Mist Cushion’, which provides decent coverage and makes the skin look glossy and moist, comes in three designs in blue, yellow and pink while the brand’s ‘Rouge Holic’, ‘Eyeshadow palette’ and ‘Face designing blusher’ products also benefit from Hera’s collaboration with Nicholas Kirkwood.

Shu emura x Kye Han Hee

Global cosmetic brand Shu emura has teamed up with domestic fashion designer Kye Han Hee to create their ‘KYE FOR SHU’ line. Reinventing k-fashion leader Kye Han Hee’s bold and witty street sense into cute graphic icons, ‘Shu emura’s best color package’ is set to be released in May and has already become a hot topic amongst K-brand lovers. The two products that stand out most in the upcoming collaboration are ‘Cushion Blusher’ and ‘Sheer Color Balm’, which are the first of their kind in Shu emura. The orange and pink hues are soft and just bright enough to go well with any skin tone. The key point to this collection will be the various eyebrow products that will be included as Kye Han Hee likes to emphasize eyebrows and eyeliners in her makeup looks.

Image source : SHU UEMURA X KYE

A sensuous collaboration between celebrities and fashion brands

The fashion industry has also been swept up in the collaboration trend. This is especially true this season, with the main trend being ‘collaboration with famous celebrities’. Fashion brands are winning the hearts of consumers by creating products that combine their high quality standards with the sensibility of celebrities. Here are some celebrities who have sent out a love-call to the fashion industry.

Image source : Gentle Monster X Kong Type 1 Sunglass

Image source : VINCIS X Kong HYO JIN HIYO BAG

Gentle Monster x Kong Hyo Jin & Vincis x Kong Hyo Jin

It’s undebatable that the biggest celebrity fashionista is Kong Hyo Jin! With their collaboration with shoe brand Suecomma Bonnie already a hit, Kong Hyo Jin has now branched out to sunglasses and bags. The brands she’s chosen are Gentle Monster’s ‘TYPE’ sunglasses series and Vincis’ ‘Hyo Bag’. Gentle Monster’s ‘TYPE’ sunglasses series is composed of three different sunglasses, with ‘TYPE 1’ and ‘TYPE 2’ already out, and the round-framed ‘TYPE 3’ sunglasses set to be released in May. ‘TYPE 1’ sunglasses have cat-eye-shaped half frames, and are available in both black and floral designs, opening the wallets of consumers who like to showcase their unique personalities. ‘TYPE 2’ sunglasses combine the recent trend of tinted lenses with a unique cutting line, and come in five different colors. With a design based off of the many circular bags Kong Hyo Jin uses at home to stay tidy, Vincis’ ‘Hyo Bag’ reflects the actress’ preferences for everything from design to materials, colors and accessories. The bag comes in two sizes of small and large, and the bag that features a unique pattern from tattoo artist Novo is quite captivating.

Supercomma B x Kang Seung Hyun

For the 2016 S/S season, Suecomma Bonnie’s unisex line ‘Supercomma B’ have created their ‘Hyoni Choice’ line through a collaboration with model Kang Seung Hyun, who is famous for her sense of style. The ‘Hyoni Choice’ line features sneakers and slip-on shoes, which Kang Seung Hyun loves to wear, as well as basic clothing items and accessories. The line has already released their metallic-based ‘Follow Me’ sneakers and slip-ons. Sparkling metallic material has been combined with a typographic print and beautiful colors like dark silver, pink, bronze, green and gold. If you’re sick of the sneakers and slip-ons you already own, it will be worth it to check out Supercomma B’s shoes.

Vov x Pernille Teisbaek, Alexandra Carl

Domestic fashion brand Vov is collaborating with Danish fashion power blogger and former model Pernille Teisbaek and her best friend stylist Alexandra Carl to create a twin look collection. The twin look is the hottest trend in street fashion these days, and the collection can’t help but be popular as the duo has added their own personal flair to hand-picked Vov items. Their ‘BEST FRIEND FOREVER’ campaign t-shirt, which was available in both Vov and their children’s line ‘vJunior’, is now being reprinted as the first batch sold out quickly. If you want to share a great twin look with your close friend on a sunny spring day, have a look at Vov’s collaboration collection.
