The summer heat does not only wear you off physically. Your skin is also affected. Thoroughly applying sunblock is not enough. Sunblocks literally protect your skin from ‘ultraviolet ray’ to a certain degree, but they cannot prevent your skin’s temperature from increasing. The skin easily becomes red under the sunlight and this is a signal that its temperature is increasing. When the temperature of your skin goes up, excessive sebum will be excreted, resulting in wider pores and less elastic skin. In other words, high skin temperature means your aging speed is accelerated. If you want a younger-looking skin and want to keep your skin elastic for a long time, always remember that roaming under the sunlight with a blind faith in sunblocks will only advance your skin’s aging process.

The surest way to lower your skin temperature is to stay away from the sunlight as much as possible! But it is impossible not to go out in order to keep a cool skin temperature. Hence, we highly recommended that you grab a parasol when going out. Also, spray mists from time to time, which will help lower your skin temperature. Still, mists with alcohol ingredients included will make your skin dry so check the ingredients first when choosing what mist to buy. If you are worried about what product to buy among the many mists, pay close attention. Here are some of the best moisturizing mists that will brush away your skin’s thirst and heat without any irritation.

Avene EAU THERMALE

Mist product from the French brand Avene, famous for its cosmetics containing mineral water. It is a steady seller in Korea and abroad, with distinctive features of no-scent and mineral water ingredients.

Dr.flora Violet Organic Lavender Toner

Certified by ECHO-CERT, the product is highly recommended for over-sensitive skin types. With absolutely no artificial dyes and scents, the natural lavender flower water will calm your skin. Perhaps it is a downside that you have to use the product by pouring it into empty sprayers.

Innisfree Green Tea Mineral Mist

Mist product made of Jeju’s organic green tea water. Its powerful mist-like spraying is the product’s strength, but some say it’s hard to control the amount of spraying. It also has a subtle green tea scent.

BENEFIT ULTRA RADIANCE FACIAL RE-HYDRATING MIST

The product includes magnesium PCA ingredients to calm down the skin. It sprays very fine particles. Yet, its scent resembles cucumber soap so people who are not fond of the cucumber scent need to be careful when using the product.
